100 Restaurants

This was an Information Architecture project. We were allowed to pick any topic to make a collection of. Naturally I picked food. I originally started off by tracking my Foodler account.(For those who don't know Foodler, its an application where you can order food to be delivered.) I soon decided to make it open to all the restaurants that I could remember going to with in the last four year. Turns out I went to a lot of restaurants! The graph shows 100 Restaurants, the type of food which is represented by color, how many times I have been their represent by a symbol, my top 10 favorite places, my least favorite, and the location.
