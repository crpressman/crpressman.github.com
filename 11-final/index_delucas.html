Delucas Market

This was a project I had in a package design class. The objective was to pick an existing brand that we thought could use an improvement. After exploring different brands I ended up choosing Deluca’s Market because the logo they have didn’t represent them well. They take pride in being a high end store, that’s been in the same location for 100 years and they provide a modern way to enjoy old fashion recipes. For the logo I tried out many different typefaces, hand drawn type, script. In the end I went with a bull they had in the original logo which I refined and a more appropriate typeface.
